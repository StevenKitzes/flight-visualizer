Note: Hover over any point to get more detailed information about that specific flight. The more red a point is, the more aggressively that flight is descending. Likewise, the more green a point is the more aggressively it is ascending. The more white a point is, the closer to level flight.

Some flights appear to have extremely high altitude or velocity. It has been suggested on forums that these may be military or research vessels. I have no idea. They throw off the scale of the graph, I acknowledge. This is a target for future improvement (an option for you to turn off extremes, or to otherwise be able to zoom in on the graph).
